# GlowUp AI: Brand Identity (VI) Guidelines

## 1. Brand North Star: "The Digital Atelier"
GlowUp AI is more than a beauty tool; it is a premium editorial space for self-discovery. Inspired by the clean, sophisticated aesthetics of **Glossier** and **Rhode**, the brand focuses on authenticity, dewy textures, and a "less is more" philosophy.

---

## 2. Visual Foundation

### 2.1 Color Palette
The palette is designed to feel airy, spacious, and skin-centric, with soft pink accents for a feminine touch.

*   **Primary White:** `#FFFFFF` (Purity, clarity, and whitespace)
*   **Soft Rose (Accent):** `#FCE7F3` (Softness, brand recognition)
*   **Deep Charcoal (Typography):** `#303030` (Contrast and readability)
*   **Luminous Grey:** `#F9FAFB` (Subtle background differentiation)

### 2.2 Typography
We use a blend of classic serif for an editorial feel and clean sans-serif for modern utility.

*   **Primary Serif (Headlines):** **Noto Serif**
    *   *Usage:* Editorial headers, quote-style callouts.
    *   *Feel:* High-end, established, sophisticated.
*   **Primary Sans-Serif (Body & Labels):** **Inter**
    *   *Usage:* Navigation, body copy, technical data.
    *   *Feel:* Functional, clean, contemporary.

---

## 3. Imagery & Art Direction
Our visuals target young women (teens to 30s) and should always feel aspirational yet attainable.

*   **Authenticity:** Avoid heavy-handed retouching. Focus on real skin textures (pores, freckles, dewiness).
*   **Diverse Styles:** Celebrate a wide range of beauty aesthetics—from "The Ethereal" (avant-garde) to "The Minimalist" (essential).
*   **Texture focus:** Use close-ups of product textures (creams, gels, serums) to evoke a sensory experience.

---

## 4. UI/UX Principles
*   **Whitespace is Luxury:** Keep layouts uncrowded. Let the imagery breathe.
*   **Clear Call to Action:** Use the brand's Soft Rose color for primary buttons to ensure they are the first thing a user sees.
*   **Editorial Grids:** Align elements to a clear vertical grid, reminiscent of a high-end beauty magazine.
